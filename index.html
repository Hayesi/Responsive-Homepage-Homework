Monastero Santa Rosa - Italy

The culinary experience is a joyful display of world-class local dishes, wines, and ingredients,the latter of which are often sourced from the hotel. Meals are served by the pool or in the restaurant. You can hire a boat and venture along to Positano or Amalfi for the day.


The St.Regis Bahia Beach Resort in Puerto Rico is an eco-friendly property that has set the highest standard of luxury and refinement.
